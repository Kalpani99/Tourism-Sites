Lipton’s Seat

Lipton's Seat is a popular tourist destination located in Ella, Sri Lanka. It holds significant historical importance as it is associated with Sir Thomas Lipton, a renowned Scottish tea magnate. Lipton, the founder of Lipton Tea, played a vital role in establishing the tea industry in Sri Lanka during the colonial era. Lipton's Seat is a viewpoint situated atop the Haputale Mountain Range, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding tea estates and valleys. It is believed that Sir Thomas Lipton used to frequent this spot to survey his vast tea empire and enjoy the splendid scenery. The viewpoint can be reached by embarking on a scenic trek through lush tea plantations, adding to the charm of the experience. Lipton's Seat serves as a testament to the rich tea heritage of Sri Lanka and attracts tourists who seek to relish its natural beauty while immersing themselves in the history of the country's tea industry.
